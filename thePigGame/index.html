🐽 Welcome to the Pig Game! 🐽

This is a single two-Player dice game that you can play with your friend!

Every turn, a player will repeatedly rolls a dice until the player decides to "End turn" or the player rolls a 1.

  • If 1 is rolled, the current score reset to 0 and it becomes the other player's turn.
  • If another number is rolled, it is added to the player's current score and the player's turn continues.
  • If the player "End turn", their current score is added to their total score and it becomes the turn of the other player.
The first player to score 100 or higher wins!
